Escondidinho de Carne

      Surprise your family making this easy recipe of escondidinho de carne for them!

      After following this recipe you'll never forget and always surprise your guests with this deilicous brazillian plate!

Ingredients:

  • 1kg potato
  • 200g mozzarela
  • 1 onion
  • 2tsp butter
  • parsley to taste
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 full diced garlic head
  • white pepper to taste
  • olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of milk

Steps:

  1. Peel and cut in half the potatoes, and bring to a boil with water and salt.
  2. After cooked, smash the potatoes, add milk and butter, and stir until it forms a paste and reserve.
  3. In a pan, add olive oil, onion and sautee the ground beef.
  4. Season with salt, white pepper, parsley and cook until water is almost gone.
  5. Coat a oven-safe tray with half of the smashed potatoes.
  6. Cover with a layer of chesse, and after, a layer of ground beef.
  7. Repeat this process and to finish sprinkle grated cheese on top.
  8. Bake for 40 minutes.
